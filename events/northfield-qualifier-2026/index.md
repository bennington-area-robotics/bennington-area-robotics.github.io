## Northfield FTC Qualifier 2026

An official FIRST Tech Challenge Level 1 Qualifying Tournament, with advancement for VT teams to the Regional Championship.

**Date:** Sunday, February 15, 2026

**Time:** 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM overall, 11:00 AM – 4:30 PM for main events

**Location:** Norwich University, Northfield, VT \| [Venue Map](venue-map.pdf){:target="_blank"}

**Game:** [FTC DECODE](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/ftc/game-and-season){:target="_blank"} (2025-2026 season)

**Teams:** 15 teams from Vermont

**Contact:** [info@bamvt.com](mailto:info@bamvt.com?subject=Qualifier)

### What is FTC?

FIRST Tech Challenge is a robotics program for students grades 7-12. Teams design, build, and program robots to compete on a 12'x12' field in alliance-based matches.
